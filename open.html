Director of Investor Relations/Marketing

About Us

Altium Capital Management is a NY-based asset manager primarily focused on the healthcare sector. Our founder, Jacob Gottlieb, MD, CFA, PRM (CEO/CIO), has over 25 years of hedge fund investing experience, developing successful teams of investment professionals and business management staff. Our team includes medical doctors, scientific professionals, and experienced fund managers who work together to uncover unique investment insights to produce superior returns. Altium’s track record dates back to 2018 with an average return of ~30%, and Gottlieb has over 20 years of audited track record.

At Altium Capital, we look for intelligent, motivated people who want to learn and develop their careers. We pride ourselves on our rigorous fundamental research process, collaborative team approach, and firmwide integrity.

Overview of the Director of Marketing Role:

We are seeking a motivated and dynamic individual to join our team and drive our capital-raising efforts. You will play a crucial role in securing investment capital from a diverse range of investors.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute comprehensive fundraising strategies to attract investment capital
  • Identify and cultivate relationships with potential investors
  • Coordinate investor meetings, presentations, and due diligence processes
  • Communicate with investors and foster long-term relationships
  • Create fundraising strategies and tactics based on market trends and demands
  • Develop client service and investor relations process
  • Prepare/upgrade materials (investor letters, due diligence questionnaires, presentations, and ad hoc requests)

Qualifications and Skills:

  • 7+ years of experience in fundraising / investor relations in alternative assets, preferably within the hedge fund industry (healthcare a plus)
  • Proven track record of successfully raising capital for hedge funds
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Professional, self-starter, collaborative, and strong attention to detail

Please send resume to recruiting@altiumcap.com with “DIRECTOR OF MARKETING” in subject line.

Biotechnology Analyst

Overview of the Biotechnology Analyst Role:

We are seeking a fundamental research analyst to join our team. Candidate must have buyside investment research experience covering therapeutics/biotechnology companies. Candidate will be responsible for identifying and researching investment opportunities (both long and short), monitoring companies/events, tracking market dynamics, reporting general economic and industry-specific developments and trends, etc.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Analyze and identify investment opportunities by interpreting complex medical, scientific and technical data (memos, checklists, and financial/scientific due diligence)
  • Track and update portfolio company and industry news/events
  • Manage internal valuation models, dashboards, and universe tracking
  • Interface with investor relations, companies, KOL’s, conferences, roadshows, etc.

Qualifications and Skills:

  • Undergraduate degree (relevant advanced degree is a plus)
  • 3+ years hedge fund investment experience covering biotechnology/therapeutics
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills; ability to clearly pitch ideas
  • Financial and data analysis, including financial statements and modeling, market and pricing estimates, and clinical data
  • Professional, self-starter, collaborative, and strong attention to detail and process

Healthcare Syndicate Analyst

Overview of the Healthcare Syndicate Analyst Role:

We are seeking a healthcare syndicate analyst to support our senior investment professionals by sourcing and diligencing public and private investment opportunities (including syndicated and privately negotiated offerings), monitoring portfolio companies, and tracking market transactions. Ideal candidate will have a financial services background in biotechnology, specialty pharmaceuticals, medical technology, or related areas. Candidate should be an eager, self-starter who can manage all aspects of the ECM syndicate process, including 1x1 interaction with management teams, roadshows, deal color, managing street relationships, all the way through to deal allocation.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Identify and analyze potential ECM investment opportunities (financing screens, deal calendar, self-sourced transactions)
  • Monitor ECM market performance and terms, build and manage valuation models, and compose investment memos
  • Track and update portfolio companies and industry news/events
  • Source new investment opportunities through frequent and direct outreach to sellside bankers and capital markets teams, corporate investor relations and management teams, conferences, roadshows, etc.
  • Assist in the execution of privately negotiated financings where appropriate

Qualifications and Skills:

  • 3+ years of healthcare investment banking experience (background in ECM a plus)
  • Proficient in financial and data analysis, including creating financial models, market and pricing estimates, etc.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills; ability to clearly pitch ideas
  • High EQ and ability to manage counterparty relationships
  • Disciplined process, highly motivated, professional, strong attention to detail
  • Legal background and/or experience structuring bespoke deals a plus

Please send resume to recruiting@altiumcap.com with “HEALTHCARE SYNDICATE ANALYST” in subject line.
